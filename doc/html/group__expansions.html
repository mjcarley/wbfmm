Detailed Description

Generation of regular and singular expansions and evaluation of them at field points.

The functions described here handle spherical harmonic expansions of complex variables, solutions of the Helmholtz equations. Expansions of real variables are dealt with in a separate set of functions, for the Laplace equation (Evaluation of the Laplace potential). The expansion coefficients are packed in single- or double-precision arrays with the index of coefficient , given by . Coefficients are represented as real and imaginary parts, so that the coefficient is given by array entries where is the index, is a stride allowing interleaved packing of data, and is an array entry.
